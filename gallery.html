Our Machinery

Mobile Crane - Our mobile cranes are versatile and can be easily transported between locations providing efficiency in operations and flexibility. They are used in construction projects for lifting and moving heavy materials on construction sites and in industrial installations.

Excavator - Perfect for digging,trenching and earthmoving tasks.

Loader - Efficient for loading and transporting materials.

Forklift -Facilitate easy movement, lifting and stacking of goods in warehouses and construction sites.

Hiab Trucks - They come equipped with cranes to allow loading and unloading heavy goods efficiently. They are also used in transportation of equipment and materials between project sites.

Cherry Picker - Allow safe and efficient access to high areas making working and maintenance at elevated heights easier.

Dozer - Used for pushing soil, sand, and debris. Our staff use them to prepare sites, road building and clearing land for earthmoving.

Backhoe - Versatile machines with digging buckets and backhoes. They are used for excavation, trenching and lifting tasks.

Roller - Essential for road construction and paving by compacting soil, gravel and asphalt.

Grader - Used to create a smooth and level surface by ensuring proper grading and drainage

Lowloader - These are specialized trailers designed to transport oversized or heavy loads.

Trailer - Transport goods in standardized containers ensuring there's efficient handling, storage and transport of materials.

Lorries - Used in the transportation of loose cargo
